About

My name is Ruby, aged 23 and I am a professional model working for JustKHOO Modelling Agency. Here is my professional portfolio link.

For the past two years, I have undertaken a wide range of modelling assignments including designer fashion, runway shows, lingerie, swimwear, sportswear, along with themed shoots, TV commercials and instructional videos. I also perform various acting roles in stage plays and in marketing campaigns.

I really enjoy creative modelling projects exploring abstract, social and artistic themes.

When modelling, I am confident and dynamic in front of a camera, displaying a vibrant set of poses and facial expressions enabling me to passionately retell stories, promptly establish rapport with colleagues and respond quickly to directions from photographers and directors.
